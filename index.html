ENVIRONMENTAL MONITORING INFORMATION SYSTEMS of THE STATE of KUWAIT (eMISK)

ENVIRONMENTAL ATLAS of THE STATE of KUWAIT

This atlas reflects an applied approach to the eMISK data. This approach highlights particular eMISK datasets shown through cartographic maps and accompanying infographics. This is not meant to be a comprehensive environmental atlas using all available data from the eMISK catalog, but rather its primary purpose is to illustrate the effective application of selected eMISK datasets in highlighting both environmental challenges and opportunities that the eMISK mission can affect. The overarching concept of the Atlas is to demonstrate the value of eMISK in protecting and serving the environment of Kuwait through highlighting an example in each environmental domain. There are 11 maps that reflect the thematic environmental data featured in the eMISK Geodatabase. Each environmental domain map and data can be accessed by clicking the buttons below.
